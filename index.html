About Us

We are proud to be among the marketing leaders in provision of quality service In Sales, Letting And property management in Kenya. Our company was established as JOCHAO LINKSHOME LTD Mombasa Kenya with Operations in Coastal Kenya and its environment. We are duly registered practicing Land and Estate Agents In Accordance With Estates agents ACT CaP: 533 of laws of Kenya. We are well experienced in the field for over 10 years, the experience we have gained over the years, and our desire is to maintain leadership in the market has been the driving force in our achievements.
